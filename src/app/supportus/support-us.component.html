Support us

MakerKing is a small indie game driven by passion. The game is free to play and contains no ads. If you like MakerKing and want to encourage its development, feel free to support us! Here are the different ways you can help the project grow.

Translation

If you are fluent in english and in another language (except for french), you can help us translate the game to your language. Join us on discord to start collaborating with us on translation.

Code contributions

For volunteers that want to get involved with fixing bugs or implementing small features for MakerKing, we are open to collaboration and code submissions. It is recommended that you have some experience with Java, libGDX or game development. Join us on discord to discuss about this.

Music track submission

Do you want your music to be in MakerKing? If your track is a good match for the game and you are willing to provide us the (non exclusive) rights to your music for free, we will add your track to our game, with credits.

Donations

The current cost of operating MakerKing's servers is $26.51 canadian dollars per month. All donations, no matter the amount, are appreciated.
To faciliate donations and to offer rewards to people who donate, a microtransaction system has been added to the game. People who donate receive frisbee coins, an in-game currency that allows to buy cosmetics to decorate your player.

In this community we take the fairness of gameplay seriously. We guarantee that cosmetics bought with frisbee coins provide no competitive advantage and never will. This is important for us. If we ever break this promise, you can expect to get a refund.

To buy coins, login into the game, click on the account menu (your head in the top right), open the shop and click on the Buy coins button on the bottom left. This will open a different shop based on whether you are playing with Steam or from itch.io (paid from Paypal). Each platform takes it's own % of your purchase, it is worth using the itch.io version of the game for cheaper prices.
